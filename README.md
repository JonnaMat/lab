## About

This spatial canvas presents my research and projects on **LLM optimization** and **edge AI**. Move around the canvas to explore different areas of work — papers, blog posts, case studies, and GitHub projects are arranged spatially to show connections and relationships.

**Explore topics:**
- LLM Inference Acceleration
- Edge AI & Model Compression
- Vision Transformer Optimization
- vLLM Plugin Development
